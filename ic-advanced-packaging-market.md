<p><p>The IC advanced packaging market includes 3D and 2.5D packaging solutions, which enhance semiconductor performance and integration. 3D packaging involves stacking multiple silicon dies vertically, allowing for higher density and improved signal integrity, while 2.5D packaging utilizes a silicon interposer to connect multiple chips side by side, facilitating faster communication and reduced power consumption. Both types are crucial in meeting the demands of advanced applications such as artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, and mobile devices, driving innovation and efficiency in semiconductor design.</p></p>
