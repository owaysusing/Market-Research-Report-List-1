<p><p>Camera inspection systems are categorized into two main types: 2D and 3D inspection systems. </p><p>2D inspection systems utilize flat images to detect surface defects, measure dimensions, and verify component alignment. They are cost-effective and widely used in industries like electronics and packaging. </p><p>In contrast, 3D inspection systems capture depth information, providing a detailed view of an object’s surface and shape. This technology is ideal for complex geometries and applications in automotive and aerospace, ensuring higher accuracy and thorough quality control.</p></p>
