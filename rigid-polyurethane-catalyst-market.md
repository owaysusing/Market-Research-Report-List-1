<p><p>The rigid polyurethane catalyst market is categorized into two main types: non-reactive amine catalysts and reactive amine catalysts. Non-reactive amine catalysts facilitate the polyurethane reaction without becoming part of the final product, enhancing processing speeds. In contrast, reactive amine catalysts chemically participate in the reaction, contributing to the final composition and properties of the polyurethane foam. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing production efficiency and achieving desired performance characteristics in rigid polyurethane applications.</p></p>
