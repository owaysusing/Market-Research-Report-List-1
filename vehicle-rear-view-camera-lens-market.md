<p><p>The vehicle rear view camera lens market consists primarily of two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high-quality image output and low light performance, making them ideal for clear visibility in various lighting conditions. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and reduced manufacturing costs, making them a popular choice for modern vehicles. Both technologies enhance safety by providing drivers with improved visibility while reversing.</p></p>
