<p><ul><li>Overflow Hole Shock Absorber</li><li>Annular Hole Shock Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic shock absorber market encompasses various types, including overflow hole shock absorbers and annular hole shock absorbers. Overflow hole shock absorbers utilize a passage that allows fluid to escape during compression, enhancing responsiveness and comfort. In contrast, annular hole shock absorbers feature multiple circular holes, facilitating better fluid flow and improved damping characteristics. Both types are designed to optimize vehicle stability and handling, providing a smoother ride by effectively managing impacts and vibrations from road surfaces.</p></p>
