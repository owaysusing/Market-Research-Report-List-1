<p><p>The 3D depth sensor market is primarily categorized into three types: infrared, laser, and others. Infrared sensors use light waves to measure distances, commonly found in devices like smartphones and gaming consoles. Laser sensors, utilizing focused laser beams, offer high accuracy and are often used in industrial automation and robotics. The "others" category encompasses technologies such as structured light and time-of-flight sensors, which employ alternative methods for depth perception, catering to diverse applications across sectors like healthcare, automotive, and robotics.</p></p>
