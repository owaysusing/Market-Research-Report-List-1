<p><p>Demand Side Platform (DSP) software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based DSPs operate on external servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and ease of integration with other digital marketing tools. They enable advertisers to manage campaigns from any location with internet access. On-premises DSPs, on the other hand, are installed locally, providing increased control over data and security. However, they may require higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts, making them suitable for organizations prioritizing data privacy and customization.</p></p>
