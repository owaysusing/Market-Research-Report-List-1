<p><p>The offshore drilling market consists of various types of rigs, primarily classified into jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile platforms with legs that rest on the seabed, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are floating structures anchored to the seabed, offering stability in deeper waters. Drillships are highly mobile vessels equipped for deep-sea drilling, allowing for operations in the most challenging environments. Each type is designed to suit specific drilling conditions and water depths, enhancing exploration and production capabilities.</p></p>
