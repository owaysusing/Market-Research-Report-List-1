<p><strong>The Electric Clothes Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vented Electric Clothes Dryer</li><li>Non-Vented Electric Clothes Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric clothes dryer market is primarily divided into two types: vented and non-vented electric clothes dryers. Vented electric dryers expel hot, moist air outside through a vent, requiring a proper ducting system. They are generally faster and more energy-efficient but can be less versatile in installation. Non-vented electric dryers, such as condenser dryers, recycle and condense moisture within the unit, making them suitable for spaces without external venting. They are often more compact and flexible in placement, catering to urban living.</p></p>
