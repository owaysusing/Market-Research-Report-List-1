<p><p>The semiconductor equipment market is divided into two primary segments: front-end equipment and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment involves processes that transform raw silicon wafers into semiconductor chips, including lithography, etching, and deposition tools. This stage focuses on creating the integrated circuits. In contrast, back-end equipment encompasses processes such as packaging, testing, and assembly of the finished semiconductor devices. This phase ensures that chips are properly encased and functional for integration into various electronic systems.</p></p>
