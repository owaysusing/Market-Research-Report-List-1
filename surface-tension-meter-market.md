<p><ul><li>Static Surface Tension Meter</li><li>Dynamic Surface Tension Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface tension meter market is segmented into two main types: static surface tension meters and dynamic surface tension meters. Static surface tension meters measure the surface tension of liquids at rest, providing consistent values for applications like quality control. Dynamic surface tension meters, on the other hand, evaluate surface tension changes during processes such as spreading or foaming, making them ideal for assessing surfactant behavior in real-time. Each type serves unique needs across various industries, including pharmaceuticals and environmental testing.</p></p>
