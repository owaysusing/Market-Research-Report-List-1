<p><p>Myocarditis, an inflammation of the heart muscle, requires timely diagnosis and effective treatment options to manage symptoms and prevent complications. Diagnosis typically involves a combination of clinical evaluation, imaging techniques like echocardiography or MRI, and laboratory tests including blood markers such as troponins and inflammatory markers. Advanced diagnostic methods may include endomyocardial biopsy, which helps confirm the inflammation's cause.</p><p>Treatment for myocarditis focuses on addressing the underlying cause, such as viral infections or autoimmune conditions, and managing symptoms. Standard approaches include administering anti-inflammatory medications, immunosuppressive therapy, and supportive care for heart function, such as diuretics and beta-blockers. In severe cases, advanced therapies, including mechanical circulatory support or heart transplantation, may be necessary.</p><p>The Myocarditis Treatment and Diagnosis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period.
