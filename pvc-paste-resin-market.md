<p><strong>The PVC Paste Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC paste resin market is primarily categorized into two production methods: the micro suspension method and the emulsion method. The micro suspension method involves dispersing PVC in a liquid medium under controlled conditions, resulting in fine particles with enhanced stability and processing characteristics. In contrast, the emulsion method utilizes surfactants to create a stable blend of PVC in water, allowing for versatile applications in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Both methods contribute to diverse product formulations and performance attributes in the market.</p></p>
