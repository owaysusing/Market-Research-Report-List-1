<p><p>Glass forming production lines consist of various methods tailored for specific products. Press lines shape thick glass using molds. Spinning lines create symmetrical items by spinning molten glass. Blow-blow lines produce hollow glass containers through a two-step blowing process. Press-blow lines combine pressing and blowing for intricate designs. Tubing lines form glass tubes for industrial uses. Flat glass lines manufacture sheets for construction and automotive applications. Other methods encompass additional specialized techniques catering to diverse glass products.</p></p>
