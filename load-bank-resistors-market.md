<p><p>The load bank resistors market is categorized into three main types: resistive, reactive, and resistive+reactive. Resistive load banks primarily simulate steady-state loads, generating heat for testing generators and UPS systems. Reactive load banks introduce inductive or capacitive components to evaluate the response of power sources under varying conditions. The resistive+reactive category combines both elements, providing a comprehensive testing solution that mimics real-world scenarios, ensuring equipment reliability and performance across diverse operating conditions.</p></p>
