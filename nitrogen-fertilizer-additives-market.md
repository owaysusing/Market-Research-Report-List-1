<p><strong>The Nitrogen Fertilizer Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Urease Inhibitors Based Nitrogen Fertilizer Additives</li><li>Nitrification Inhibitor Based Nitrogen Fertilizer Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrogen fertilizer additives enhance the efficiency of nitrogen use in agriculture. Urease inhibitors slow down the conversion of urea into ammonia, reducing nitrogen loss and promoting better plant absorption. Nitrification inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent the conversion of ammonium into nitrate, minimizing leaching and volatilization losses. Both types of additives contribute to improved crop yields, reduced environmental impact, and optimized nitrogen management, making them essential in modern agricultural practices focused on sustainability and productivity.</p></p>
