<p><p>The laparoscope and endoscope market includes flexible and rigid endoscopes, each designed for specific medical applications. Flexible endoscopes are characterized by their maneuverability, allowing them to navigate through the body's flexible paths, making them ideal for gastrointestinal, respiratory, and urological procedures. Rigid endoscopes, on the other hand, are inflexible and used primarily in surgical applications such as arthroscopy and laparoscopy, providing high stability and clarity for direct visualization during procedures. Both types contribute significantly to minimally invasive diagnostics and treatments.</p></p>
