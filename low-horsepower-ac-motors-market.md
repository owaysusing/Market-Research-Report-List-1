<p><strong>The Low Horsepower AC Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Asynchronous Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low horsepower AC motors market primarily includes synchronous and asynchronous motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed and are synchronized with the supply frequency, ideal for applications requiring precise speed control. Asynchronous motors, or induction motors, function on the principle of electromagnetic induction and are commonly used due to their simplicity and reliability. The choice between these types depends on application needs, energy efficiency, and operational requirements, driving their demand in various industries like manufacturing and HVAC systems.</p></p>
