<p><p>The white pigment market is categorized into three main types: inorganic pigments, organic pigments, and specialty pigments. Inorganic pigments, such as titanium dioxide and zinc oxide, are widely used for their excellent opacity and durability. Organic pigments, derived from carbon-based compounds, offer vibrant colors but may have lower opacity. Specialty pigments are tailored for specific applications, including effects like pearlescence or fluorescence, catering to niche industries such as cosmetics and specialty coatings, providing unique visual properties.</p></p>
