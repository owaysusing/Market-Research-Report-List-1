<p><p>A Positive Input Ventilation (PIV) System is an energy-efficient ventilation solution designed to improve indoor air quality by introducing fresh air into a building. <p><p>The Positive Input Ventilation (PIV) system market includes two primary types: Whole House PIV and Loft Mounted PIV. Whole House PIV systems are designed to provide consistent ventilation throughout an entire residence, improving indoor air quality and promoting a healthier living environment. Loft Mounted PIV systems, on the other hand, are installed in loft spaces and utilize the natural stack effect to distribute fresh air throughout the home, making them an efficient option for specific residential layouts. Both types enhance air exchange and reduce humidity.</p></p>
