<p><p>The Exhaust Oxygen Sensor market primarily comprises two types: Titanium Oxide and Zirconia types. Titanium Oxide sensors function by detecting oxygen levels through changes in electrical resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Zirconia sensors, widely used in automotive applications, utilize a zirconium dioxide ceramic that generates a voltage in response to oxygen concentration differences, ensuring efficient exhaust gas monitoring. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions in vehicles, catering to environmental regulations.</p></p>
