<p><p>The TIG welder market is segmented into various types including pulsed-current and dabber welders. Pulsed-current TIG welders provide a controlled welding arc through variable electrical input, allowing for improved precision and minimal distortion, ideal for thin materials. Dabber welders, on the other hand, focus on a manual technique that utilizes a dipping action, often preferred for artistic or specialized applications. Each type caters to different user needs, skill levels, and project requirements, contributing to a diverse market landscape.</p></p>
