<p><strong>The Facial Motion Capture System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Motion Capture</li><li>Mechanical Motion Capture</li><li>Optical Motion Capture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Facial motion capture systems are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Magnetic Motion Capture** utilizes electromagnetic sensors to track facial movements, providing accurate 3D positioning but can be affected by magnetic interference.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Mechanical Motion Capture** employs physical rigs and sensors attached to the face, offering detailed movement capture but requiring extensive setup and can restrict natural motion.</p><p>   </p><p>3. **Optical Motion Capture** uses cameras and markers to capture facial expressions in real-time, delivering high precision without physical attachments, making it popular in film and gaming industries.</p></p>
