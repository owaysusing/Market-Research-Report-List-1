<p><ul><li>Supported Spindles</li><li>Bottom Whorl Drop Spindles</li><li>Top Whorl Drop Spindles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The short-staple spindles market consists of various types, primarily supported spindles, bottom whorl drop spindles, and top whorl drop spindles. Supported spindles are designed to rest on a surface, providing stability while spinning. Bottom whorl drop spindles feature a weight at the bottom, promoting faster spinning and easier control. In contrast, top whorl drop spindles have the weight positioned at the top, allowing for a more versatile spinning technique, making them popular among hand spinners for their ease of use.</p></p>
