<p><p>The wound care market features various medical device technologies designed for effective healing. Foam dressings provide cushioning and absorb excess exudate. Transparent films offer a protective, non-absorptive barrier. Hydrofibers enhance moisture retention, while hydrogels hydrate dry wounds. Collagen dressings support tissue regeneration. Other types include antimicrobial dressings and specialty products tailored for specific wound care needs, enhancing treatment outcomes.</p></p>
