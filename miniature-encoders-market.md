<p><p>The miniature encoders market is categorized into rotary and linear encoders. Rotary encoders convert the angular position of a shaft into an electronic signal, widely used in robotics and automation for precise motion control. Linear encoders, on the other hand, measure linear position or movement, ensuring accuracy in applications such as CNC machines and manufacturing processes. Both types are compact, catering to the growing demand for space-efficient solutions in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
