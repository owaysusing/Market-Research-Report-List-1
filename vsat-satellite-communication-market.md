<p><strong>The VSAT Satellite Communication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Star Structure</li><li>Mesh Structure</li><li>Star and Mesh Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) satellite communication market is categorized into three key structures: Star, Mesh, and a combination of both. </p><p>In the Star structure, multiple remote terminals connect to a central hub, facilitating straightforward communication but with a single point of failure. The Mesh structure interconnects terminals directly, enhancing redundancy and resilience through multiple communication paths. The Star and Mesh combined structure offers flexibility, allowing efficient communication alongside robustness, making it suitable for various applications and requirements in satellite communication.</p></p>
