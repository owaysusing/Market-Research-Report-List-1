<p><p>The chemotherapy syringe pump market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel syringe pumps are designed to administer medication through one syringe, providing focused drug delivery for individual patients. In contrast, multi-channel syringe pumps can accommodate multiple syringes, allowing the simultaneous administration of different drugs or varying dosages. This versatility is beneficial in complex treatment regimens, particularly for oncology patients requiring personalized care. Both types enhance the efficiency and precision of chemotherapy administration in clinical settings.</p></p>
