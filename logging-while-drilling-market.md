<p><p>Logging While Drilling (LWD) encompasses various technologies that enhance the efficiency and accuracy of subsurface exploration. Gamma Ray Systems measure natural radioactivity to identify lithology and stratigraphy. Resistivity Systems assess the electrical properties of formations to evaluate hydrocarbon presence. Formation Pressure Systems provide data on pore pressures, aiding in drilling decisions. Imaging Systems create detailed visual representations of geological formations. Other systems may include features like electromagnetic sensors or sonic tools, collectively contributing to improved drilling performance and resource management.</p></p>
