<p><p>The Plum Oil market can be categorized into three types based on extraction methods: Cold Press, Virgin Method, and Solvent Extraction. Cold Press involves mechanically extracting oil from plums without heat, preserving flavor and nutrients. The Virgin Method also emphasizes minimal processing, focusing on high-quality oil from fresh plums. Solvent Extraction, on the other hand, utilizes chemical solvents to maximize yield but may compromise some quality. Each method serves different consumer preferences and industrial applications while influencing oil quality and market dynamics.</p></p>
