<p><p>The optical lenses market comprises primarily two types: resin lenses and optical glass lenses. Resin lenses are lightweight and impact-resistant, making them popular in eyewear, particularly for high-index options. They offer versatility in design and can be produced in various shapes and sizes. In contrast, optical glass lenses provide superior optical clarity and scratch resistance, often preferred in high-end applications such as photography and scientific instruments. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, driving innovation and competition in the market.</p></p>
